Threat Report: APT-Sample Targets Financial Services

Summary
-------
Our research team has tracked a new intrusion campaign attributed to the
threat actor APT-Sample, primarily targeting the financial services and
banking sectors across several regions.

Initial access was achieved through spearphishing emails carrying malicious
attachments (MITRE ATT&CK T1566.001). Once executed, the implant established
command-and-control over standard web protocols (T1071.001).

Indicators of Compromise
------------------------
The following synthetic indicators were observed during the investigation:

  - Command-and-control IP:   198.51.100.23
  - Staging domain:           staging.malicious.example
  - Payload delivery URL:     http://malicious.example/update/payload.bin
  - Dropper (SHA-256):        e3b0c44298fc1c149afbf4c8996fb92427ae41e4649b934ca495991b7852b855

Defenders should treat all of the above as malicious and block accordingly.
